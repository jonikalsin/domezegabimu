Lil Bibby Biography

Lil Bibby is best known as Rapper, Songwriter who has an estimated Net Worth of $6 Million. Hip hop and rap artist best known for his mixtapes Free Crack, Free Crack II, and Free Crack III. He was born on July 18,1994, Chicago, IL. He was selected to the 2014 XXL Freshman class list after Young Thug had to drop out at the last minute. Born Brandon Dickerson, he released his first mixtape Free Crack in 2013 after becoming active in the music industry in 2012. He collaborated with fellow Chicago based rappers Lil Herb and King L on his debut mixtape. He has brothers, George and Shawn.  His zodiac sign is Cancer
