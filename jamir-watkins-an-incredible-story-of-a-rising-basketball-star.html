Jamir Watkins: An Incredible Story of a Rising Basketball Star

One of the most impressive emerging college basketball stars. We take a closer look at this incredible athlete, the life and career, path to success, and the ripples that Jamir Watkins has sent through the sport.


Early Life and Background

Jamir Watkins was born July 6, 2001, in Trenton, New Jersey, his home city was a breeding ground for basketball. He grew up going to school at Trenton Catholic Academy in Hamilton, New Jersey, and had a genuine love for the sport. In his formative years, Jordan exhibited tremendous commitment and labor to help his future achievements on the basketball court.


Florida State Seminoles College Career

Jamir Watkins found a new home at Florida State where he thrived for the Seminoles. His impact has been immense as a forward for the team, providing valuable contributions that helped the team immensely. Watkins comes in at a daunting 6'7" and weighs 210 lbs, which gives him the physical attributes that have made him a dynamic weapon for the Seminoles.
2023-24 Season Stats
Points: 15.6
Rebounds: 6.0
Assists: 2.8

As you can see, these stats identify him in scoring, rebounds, and passing. His play led to him catching the eyes of the voters, as evidenced by him making the Sunshine Slam All-Tournament Team and taking home the USBWA Player of the Week award.


Impact on the Game

Watkins' on-court impact goes beyond player achievements. His size is a factor in why Florida State has performed the way it has in the ACC, as well. His role in helping another highly successful Seminole team is significant in terms of SEC power rankings, as he has been a key contributor for the Seminoles.

Having played at a high level every night has raised his stock around the college basketball world and has made him a prospect for the future of the sport.


Career path and prospects

A couple years away from his NBA Draft 2025 eligibility, Jamir Watkins finds himself on the brink of the next turn of his basketball adventure. An early entrant in the 2024 draft, he has taken his name out, indicating his desire to continue working on his game and entering college basketball before winding up in the professional ranks. His intention with his slow and measured entry in the scene just emphasized his efforts to make progress and transform into a greater player.

In the End

Jamir Watkins is the definition of a blue-chip, young basketball player who mixes skill with drive and a desire to be the best. His journey from Trenton, NJ to the courts at Florida State University captures the quest for athletic supremacy and the influence on so many young careers that the path of college basketball can have. While he still runs toward the NBA, the basketball world clamors on waiting for the next page of the Jamir Watkins Cinderella Story.
