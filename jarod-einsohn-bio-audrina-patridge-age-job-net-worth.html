Meet Jarod Einsohn, Audrina Patridge Boyfriend

Jarod Einsohn and Audrina Patridge officially started dating in mid-2022. However, the producer only made their relationship IG official in October of that year, while even months later Audrina had yet to inform her fans.

The two were last spotted enjoying a date night at the premiere of Glass Onion. Obviously, Jarod documented the outing on IG by posting a boomerang of Audrina running up to kiss him on the cheek.

Prior to Audrina, Jarod famously dated the actress Jennifer Love Hewitt. The two were first captured in August 2011 as they stepped out for a Japanese at Katsuya in Los Angeles.

The news about Jarod and Jennifer came just a few months after Jennifer split with her previous boyfriend Alex Beh.

As for Jarod’s now girlfriend Audrina’s past relationships, she was married to Corey Bohan for one year before they split in 2017. Allegedly, Audrina faced a “violent outburst” from her ex-husband with whom she also shares a daughter named Kirra.

“I needed to close a door so I could keep moving forward in my life and not look back,” Audrina said at the time.

Before Corey, Audrina dated Justin Bobby Brescia (2006 – 2010, on and off), Ryan Cabrera (in 2010), and Nic Roland (in April 2011). However, in fact, Audrina had been dating Corey on and off ever since 2008.

Trivia: Jarod might be in a relationship with Audrina. But he had yet to update his relationship status on FB. It read “single” the last we checked.
