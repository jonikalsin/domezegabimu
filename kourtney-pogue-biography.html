Kourtney Pogue Bio (Wiki)

Who is Kourtney Pogue?

Kourtney Pogue is an American actress, businesswoman, former Los Angeles Kings Ice, and model.

Kourtney Pogue is popularly known as the wife of  Justin Turner an American professional baseball third baseman Los Angeles Dodgers of Major League Baseball.

In addition, she got hype for her interpretation of Bikini Party Girl in the Tv comedy film, Not Another High School Show.

What are the birth facts of Kourtney Pogue?

Kourtney Pogue was born on 31 October 1986 in Chesterton, Indiana, USA. As of 2022, her age is 35 years.

She was raised by her father Anthony Pogue and her mother Tina McDonald Pogue. Unfortunately, her father passed away at the age of 67 because of natural causes.

Likewise, she has elder siblings namely Ryan Pogue and Kristin Pogue.

She holds the citizenship of America and her birth sign is Scorpio. Moreover, she graduated from Chesterton High School in 2005 and completed her studies in psychology at the University of California, Los Angeles in 2013.

Career, Professional Life

Talking about her professional life, she has made a career for herself in various fields such as acting, television appearances, modeling, cheerleading, and philanthropic activities.

She is renowned as the vice president of the Justin Turner Foundation which supports homeless veterans.

She started her career as a cheerleader and also competed in the Miss Teen Indiana pageant. In 2006, she made her acting debut in the tv series “Untold Stories of the ER” and later had a brief role in the Tv comedy film, Not Another High School Show.

Moreover, she also photoshoots in sexy poses with bikinis and uploads them on the website ‘Kourtneyereppert.’

Furthermore, the husband and wife ran a podcast show, ‘Holiday Kourt’ on Netflix during the COVID-19 pandemic.
