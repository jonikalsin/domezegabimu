Hugh Jackman congratulates golf star Cam Smith on winning the British Open by comparing mullets

He’s a huge sports fans and all-round good guy.

And on Tuesday, Hugh Jackman paid tribute to Australia’s latest golfing champ Cam Smith in a hilarious Instagram post.

The 53-year-old Wolverine legend shared a photo of himself sporting a slight mullet and wrote in the caption ‘You win Cameron Smith, on and off the field.’

Hugh Jackman paid tribute to Australia’s latest golfing champ Cam Smith in a hilarious Instagram post on Tuesday  

Hugh shared two pictures in the post. 

One featured Cam, 28, sporting his long-haired trade mark mullet during his triumphant final round at the 150th Open Golf Championships in St. Andrews, Scotland on Sunday.

The other showed a bearded Hugh styling the sleek short mullet fans know from his 2015 film, Chappie.

A quick scroll down Smith’s Instagram profile reveals plenty of images of beer, McDonald’s feeds, top catches on fishing boats, and golf’s newest superstar ripping up Aussie V8 Supercars tracks on a simulator. 

Meanwhile, Hugh is currently starring in the revival of the Broadway smash hit The Music Man in New York.

Hugh and his wife, Deborra-Lee Furness, moved to Manhattan in 2008, and they reside in the trendy West Village. 

Last year, the X-Men star revealed he was homesick for Australia after a lengthy coronavirus lockdown in NYC.

Hugh, Deborra-Lee and their children had initially been in Melbourne when Covid-19 was declared a pandemic in March 2019.

But they returned to the U.S. just four days later in order to keep the family together.

Before Covid struck, Hugh was said to be contemplating a move back to Sydney’s Bondi Beach, where he owns a $6million property.

‘Hugh has privately considered a move back for years, but he never followed through with it because he has such a good life in the States,’ a source told New Idea.

He is reportedly missing having the beach at his backdoor and feels attached to the laid-back lifestyle in Australia.

In addition to his acting career, Hugh also runs the Laughing Man Coffee company.
