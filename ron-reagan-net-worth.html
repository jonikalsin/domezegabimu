Ronald Reagan Jr Net Worth

Ronald Reagan Jr, son of former President Ronald Reagan and Nancy Reagan, has a net worth of $12 million. He dropped out of Yale after one semester to pursue his dream of becoming a ballet dancer, but later became known for his liberal views which differed from his father’s conservative political beliefs.

Reagan Jr is a former talk radio host and writer, having previously hosted a daily three-hour talk radio show and a daily show on Air America Radio. He is currently a contributor to MSNBC and has appeared in commercials supporting his atheist beliefs.

Reagan Jr was married to clinical psychologist Doria Palmieri from 1980 until her death in 2014. He currently resides in Seattle.

At the time of his mother’s death in 2016, Nancy Reagan was worth $25 million.
