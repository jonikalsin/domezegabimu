Does Ali Wong Have Asian Ancestry?

On April 19, 1982, Wong was born in Pacific Heights, San Francisco, California. She is Tom Wong and Adolphus Wong’s daughter.

In 1960, her mother Tom, a social worker, left Huế, South Vietnam, and moved to the United States.
