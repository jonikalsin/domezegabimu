Kamar de los Reyes children: Meet Michael Luis de los Reyes, John Ruben de los Reyes

Kamar de los Reyes is a Puerto Rican actor who was born on November 8, 1967. He is best known for his roles as Antonio Vega on the ABC soap opera One Life to Live and Raul Menendez in the 2012 video game Call of Duty: Black Ops II. In season four of Sleepy Hollow, he played Jobe, a demon. He is currently starring as a detective in Season 3 of The Rookie.

In a 1995 production of William Shakespeare’s The Tempest, De los Reyes co-starred with Patrick Stewart as a “dashing, if thick-tongued” Ferdinand. De los Reyes was named to People Magazine’s “Fabulous 50” list in 1997. In 2001, he appeared in The Way She Moves, a television film starring Annabeth Gish and fellow soap opera star Daniel Cosgrove. He also appeared in Toni Braxton’s “Spanish Guitar” music video and the controversial film Love and Suicide. He has appeared on several television shows, including Law & Order and CSI: Miami. In 1995, de los Reyes co-starred in Oliver Stone’s controversial biopic Nixon, alongside James Woods, Ed Harris, and Sir Anthony Hopkins, as convicted Watergate burglar Eugenio Martnez.
