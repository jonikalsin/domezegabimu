"Fruit of the Spirit" is based on Galatians 5:22-23. Through songs and activities, children experience what it means to grow and live out an authentic, joyful faith. Children learn to follow the Holy Spirit as they develop godly character and become firmly rooted and grounded in Christ's love.

This 15-week unit for Preschool (ages 3-5) includes songs, hymns, demonstration and accompaniment recordings, KIDPages, Song Teaching Steps, small- and large-groups teaching activities, colorful visuals, and step-by-step rehearsal plans.
