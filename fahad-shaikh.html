Wiki/Biography

Fahad Sheikh [1] was born on Wednesday, 22 February 1989 (age 32 years; as of 2021) in Lahore, Pakistan. His zodiac sign is Pisces. He did his schooling at Beaconhouse, Lahore, Pakistan. Later, he pursued CAT at SKANS School of Accountancy ACCA Campus for a year in Lahore. He then attended London College of Accountancy, London, to continue pursuing ACCA. He holds a diploma in filmmaking from the Hollywood Film Institute. [2] [3]

Career

He started his career as a TV host in the Pakistani fashion roadshow ‘11 Number’ aired on Style 360 in 2011. He debuted as an actor in the Pakistani drama ‘Haya Kay Rung’ (2016) aired on ARY Zindagi. He has acted in various Pakistani dramas including ‘Mubarak Ho Beti Hui Hai (2017), ‘Meraas’ (2017), ‘Badbakht’ (2018), ‘Bandish’ (2019), and ‘Jalan’ (2020).

In 2020, he appeared in the digital entertainment channel See Prime Pakistani short film ‘Naam Kya Rakha?’ as Shayan.

He acted in Pakistan’s first-ever digital feature film ‘You..Me..Us’ in 2021 which is directed by Sohail Javed. Fahad is a singer too and has uploaded various music videos on YouTube.
