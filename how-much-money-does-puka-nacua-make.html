How much money does Puka Nacua make?

Puka Nacua, the talented wide receiver for the Los Angeles Rams, has secured his position as a valuable asset to the team's offense. Not only has he showcased his skills on the field, but he has also managed to secure a lucrative contract that reflects his value and potential.

Puka Nacua's Contract Details

According to recent reports, Puka Nacua signed a four-year contract with the Rams worth an impressive $4,084,977. This contract includes a signing bonus of $244,976 and an average annual salary of $1,021,244. Such a substantial contract demonstrates the team's confidence in Nacua's abilities and potential.

Nacua's Rookie Season Performance

Despite being a fifth-round pick in the 2023 NFL Draft, Puka Nacua has displayed remarkable talent and potential throughout his rookie season. He has accumulated an impressive 1,163 receiving yards, with 87 receptions and four touchdowns. These statistics highlight his impact on the Rams' offense and his ability to contribute to the team's success.
