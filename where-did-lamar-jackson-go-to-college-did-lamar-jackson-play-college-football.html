Lamar Demeatrice Jackson Jr. is an American football quarterback for the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League (NFL).

Where did Lamar Jackson go to college? Did Lamar Jackson play college football?

Lamar Jackson attended Boynton Beach High School in Boynton Beach, Florida, where he played high school football.

After high school, he committed to playing college football at the University of Louisville. At the end of his freshman season, he was named the MVP of the 2015 Music City Bowl.

In the following season, he was selected as the 2016 Heisman Trophy winner and became Louisville’s first Heisman Trophy winner in school history. In addition, he also won prestigious awards at the university.

Following his junior season, he was named the men’s ACC Athlete of the Year for all conference sports. At the end of his collegiate career, he was drafted by the Baltimore Ravens in the first round with the 32nd overall pick in the draft.
