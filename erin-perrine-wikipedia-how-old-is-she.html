Erin Perrine Wikipedia: How Old is She? Facts To Know About The Lawyer

Erin Perrine was born to her parents, Casey Callanan (father) and Karen Smith Callanan (mother). Perrine grew up with her three siblings, Eamon Eric Callanan, Christine ‘Chris’ Hickey, and Megan Callanan Lasaponara, in Rochester. Unfortunately, she lost her beloved brother Eamon, who was 28 years old at the time, to an opioid overdose. Her sister Christine is a lawyer, and Megan is a pediatrician.

Further, she obtained her education from the University of Connecticut, where she earned a bachelor’s degree in Political Science. She applied to Catholic institutions such as Christ the King elementary (now Saint Kateri School), Seton Catholic School, and Siena Catholic Academy for primary schooling.

Erin played in all the Irondequoit Sports Association games and cheered for Pop Warner in high school while pursuing her studies. Perrine interned at the Republican National Convention in 2008 while in college. She served as a press secretary for the state Assembly’s Republican caucus after college, then on the Senate campaign of Republican Ron Johnson.

Later, she worked on Rep. Paul Ryan’s (R-Wis.) 2012 campaign and was employed as press secretary for Sen. John Thune (R-SD). In 2017, she started working as a national press secretary for House Majority Leader Kevin McCarthy (R-Calif). Later, in March 2019, she was hired by the Trump reelection campaign.
