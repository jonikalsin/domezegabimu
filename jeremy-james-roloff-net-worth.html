Jeremy Roloff Wiki Biography

Jeremy Roloff, born on the 10th of May, 1990, is an American television personality, who gained fame when he and his family became the stars of the reality television show “Little People, Big World.”

So how much is Roloff’s net worth? As of late 2017, based on authoritative sources it is reported to be $300,000 acquired largely from his years appearing in the reality show “Little People, Big World”.

Jeremy James Roloff Net Worth $1 Million

Born in Hillsboro, Oregon, Roloff is the son of Matthew Roloff, an actor, writer and television personality, and Amy Roloff, also a television personality. He has a twin brother named Zach, and two other siblings named Jacob and Molly.

In 2006, Roloff and his family shot to fame when their family became the subject of the reality TV show entitled “Little People, Big World”. The show revolved on how his parents, who both have dwarfism, run their day to day lives raising their children and managing their farm house in Oregon, called the Roloff Farm.

Despite being a television personality at such a young age, Roloff continued pursuing his personal interests and passion. After graduating from high school, he later on attended the local community college in his hometown. Then with his growing passion for photography, Roloff decided to leave Oregon, and attended Brooks Institute of Photography in Southern California to polish his craft.

The Roloff family’s reality television show certainly helped to increase their net worth, but aside from being TV stars, their farm is also another source of their income. In 1990, his parents bought a 34-acre farmhouse in Helvetia, Oregon to create Roloff Farm. When his parents were able to finish the farmhouse it now includes a pirate ship on a lake, a medieval castle, a three-story house, a mine shaft, a soccer field, a volleyball court, underground tunnels, and a pumpkin farm. They opened the farmhouse to the public and have been visited by many thousands of people since then. Their pumpkins also led them to launch Pumpkin Food Product Business that also helps in their net worth.

Today, Jeremy Roloff is still appearing along with his family in “Little People, Big World.” He also works as a professional photographer.

In terms of his personal life, Roloff has been married to Audrey Mirabella Botti since 2014, and together they have a daughter named Ember Jean.

Roloff was also involved in a controversy when an article entitled “A Big Bigot in the Land of the Little People” appeared online on the National Enquirer. The article exposed Roloff and how he was caught making racial and homophobic comments against the LGBT community. His parents have issued an apology since then.
