Branden Grace children: Meet son Roger Grace

Branden John Grace is a professional golfer from South Africa who currently plays on the European Tour, the PGA Tour, and the Sunshine Tour.

In 2012, he became the first player in the history of the European Tour to win his first four European Tour titles in the same year.

He was born on May 20, 1988 (age 34 years), in Pretoria, South Africa.

Branden Grace children: Meet son Roger Grace

Branden Grace is married to his long-time girlfriend Nieke Coetzee.

The couple tied the knot in November 2016, in an intimate ceremony.

Grace and his wife, Nieke Coetzee welcomed their first child, a son, Roger on 25th April 2018.

Who is Roger’s mother?

Roger Grace is the son of Nieke Coetzee who is currently married to sweetheart Braden Grace.

Just like Braden Grace, she is also from South Africa.

Not a lot is known about Roger’s mother, Nieke. The two have been rumored to be together since 2013.
